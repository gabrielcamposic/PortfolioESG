<p>PortfolioESG is a powerful Python-based system designed to help you discover optimized investment portfolios. By leveraging historical financial data and sophisticated analysis techniques, it aims to identify asset combinations that offer the best potential risk-adjusted returns, with a focus on Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) criteria.</p>
<p>The system intelligently navigates the complexities of financial markets to align your investment strategy with both profitability and purpose. It's built for users who want to make data-driven decisions and understand the mechanics behind their portfolio choices.</p>
<p>At its core, the project consists of two main Python scripts that work in tandem: one dedicated to robust data acquisition and another to perform the intensive optimization calculations.</p>
